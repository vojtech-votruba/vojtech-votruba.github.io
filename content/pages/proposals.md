Inspired by my discussion with Lukáš about open science, I have decided to put up some research (and maybe even other) proposals that I would like to explore, preferably in collaboration with someone. Please, write me an email if you feel like we could work on some of these together.

#### Input convex neural operators
I have done some research in connecting physics and machine learning. A key architecture that I have employed were the [ICNNs](https://arxiv.org/abs/1609.07152). I would like to extend their idea from neural networks to neural operators to achive a new structure-preserving design capable of learning convex functionals.     

#### Lack-of-fit reduction for quantum master equations
In my diploma thesis, I derive some quantum master equations. I would like to use the [lack-of-fit reduction](https://dspace.cuni.cz/handle/20.500.11956/181823?show=full) proceedure to either rederive some of the existing equations or to find new ones with some interesting properites.
